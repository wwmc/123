ABOUT US

The Mission


We are passionate about social inclusion.

Our Vision


A world where everyone matters, where the excluded can be heard. We also believe in Peace justice and strong institution. We believe in the power of Communication and the media for development and social change. We therefore use story telling and the power of the media to advocate for change and inclusion especially of the marginalized societies; People living with disabilities, the refugees and internally displaced persons, women and girls, and youths in Nigeria.

Our Objectives


  1. To create awareness of the problem and sensitize.
  2. To make the target audience of listeners take action by supporting our projects towards the excluded.
  3. To influence policy change especially such that will favour the marginalized society.
